Black Truffle Mushroom Gnocchi

Description:

A rich, creamy, earthy pasta dish, characterized by its tangy parmesan, toothful gnocchi, and with just the right amount of black truffle to accentuate the mushroom.

Ingredients:

  • 24 oz gnocchi
  • 3 tbsp black truffle butter
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • 16 oz mushroom, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan, shredded
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • fresh thyme, to garnish
  • red pepper flakes, to garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare gnocchi according to package directions.
  2. Meanwhile, melt Black Truffle Butter in a large skillet or sauce pan over medium heat. Add shallots and mushrooms, and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add cream and Parmesan cheese. Cook, stirring frequently, 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  4. Put cooked gnocchi into the sauce and stir to combine.
  5. Salt and pepper, to taste, and add fresh thyme and red pepper flakes, if desired.
  6. Serve.
